Hello!

CORS Planner is a powerful and elegant timetable builder (unofficial) for NUS National University of Singapore and NTU Nanyang Technological University Students.

Features

  • CORS Planner uses the latest module information from NUS CORS and NTU Schedule.
  • Modules are smartly allocated onto timetable that minimize time clashes.
  • You can tell CORS Planner Timetable Builder the timeslots NOT to allocate modules (e.g. Lunch Break).
  • You can drag and drop modules in the timetable to alternative timeslots.
  • Your timetable is saved automatically for your next visit.

Support!

Share and Follow the latest CORS Planner updates!

Feedback
